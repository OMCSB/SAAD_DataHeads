Why Us?

  • First, our organisation has devised a business method that enables customers to purchase the proper ticket based on the class of travel, food selections, age group, and special services demanded.
  • In addition, we have created an automatic reporting system for employees in the management department. This is done by creating a system that can provide weekly and monthly sales statistics depending on the number of tickets sold and the profit of the tickets sold.
  • Moreover, the system presented by our organisation is also built to carry out the information system in such a way that the system adheres to extremely strict regulations based on the cancellation, the reservation, and rescheduling of tickets established by certain airlines. So, if a ticket is cancelled or amended less than four hours before the planned flight, the ticket's value will be forfeited. The ticketing system is constructed such that if a round-trip, multi-city journey has begun, the ticket must be renewed and finished using the original start date. The ticket will be reissued and finished within a year of the revised travel start date, if the travel of the ticket has not yet begun.
